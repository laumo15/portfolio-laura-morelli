Hi! My name is Laura and I'm from Milan, Italy.
I'm a career switcher. While working in the finance department at a software company, I got curious about coding. I proved my hand at it and discovered it's something I enjoy immensely. So, I decided to educate myself while maintaining my full-time job - you know, something's gotta pay the bill.

I pursued the full SheCodes curriculum, and I learned a lot about front-end development. I started with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; I became familiar with tools like VS Code, GitHub, and Figma; and I dived into exciting technologies including React.js, Bootstrap, APIs, and Responsive development. I had a blast!

In the meanwhile, I also started researching further resources. For instance, I spent the time leading up to 2023 with a daily Javascriptmas challenge. It was a lot of fun and incredibly useful to work on smaller, more specific challenges and practice daily.

Now, it's time for me to challenge myself and make coding my career.

As a career switcher, I may lag a bit behind on technical experience. But I bring with me everything that I've learned in the past years at work: Communicating with colleagues from any background and culture, organizing work for myself and my team, collaborating with others towards a common goal, and just being a nice person to work with - or so my colleagues say!

I can't wait to see what the future has in store for me!
